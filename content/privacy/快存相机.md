快存相机（SnapSaver Camera）尊重并保护用户隐私。本隐私政策说明快存相机如何处理您在使用本应用时涉及的信息。

## 信息收集

快存相机不要求您注册账户，也不会收集您的姓名、电子邮件地址、电话号码、精确位置、设备标识符或其他可用于识别您身份的信息。

本应用不集成广告、用户行为分析或跨应用追踪服务，也不会将您的照片或个人数据上传至开发者服务器。

## 照片与本地数据

快存相机使用设备相机拍摄照片，并按照您选择的位置将照片保存到设备本地。照片的处理与存储均在您的设备上完成。

您创建的保存按钮、保存路径和应用设置等配置信息仅存储在设备本地，用于提供应用功能。卸载应用或在系统设置中清除应用数据后，这些配置可能会被删除；已经保存到相册或文件目录中的照片需由您自行管理和删除。

## 设备权限

为提供核心功能，本应用可能请求以下系统权限：

- **相机权限**：用于取景和拍摄照片。
- **照片或存储权限**：用于将照片保存到您选择的相册或目录，以及访问您主动选择的保存位置。

您可以随时在设备的系统设置中撤回这些权限。撤回权限后，与该权限相关的功能可能无法正常使用。

## 数据共享与第三方服务

快存相机不会出售、出租或共享您的个人数据。

本应用可能提供指向项目主页等外部网站的链接。当您主动打开外部链接时，将由相应网站按照其自身的隐私政策处理相关信息。

## 数据保留与删除

由于快存相机不会把您的照片或个人数据传输到开发者服务器，开发者不会在服务器上保留此类数据。您可以在设备上删除已拍摄的照片，也可以通过卸载应用或清除应用数据来删除应用的本地配置。

## 儿童隐私

快存相机不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。由于本应用不收集或上传个人数据，其数据处理方式对所有用户一致。

## 隐私政策更新

如果应用功能或数据处理方式发生变化，本政策可能会更新。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并修改页面顶部的生效日期。

## Privacy Policy (English)

Effective date: June 12, 2026

SnapSaver Camera respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

### Information Collection

SnapSaver Camera does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, precise location, device identifiers, or other information that can identify you.

The app does not include advertising, behavioral analytics, or cross-app tracking services. It does not upload your photos or personal data to the developer's servers.

### Photos and Local Data

SnapSaver Camera uses your device's camera to take photos and saves them locally in the location you select. Photo processing and storage take place on your device.

Saver buttons, destination paths, and app preferences are stored only on your device to provide app functionality. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data may remove these settings. Photos already saved to your photo library or file directories must be managed and deleted by you.

### Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

- **Camera**: to display the camera preview and take photos.
- **Photos or storage**: to save photos to an album or directory you select and to access a destination you explicitly choose.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings. Features that depend on a revoked permission may stop working.

### Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

SnapSaver Camera does not sell, rent, or share your personal data.

The app may contain links to external websites, such as the project's source-code page. If you choose to open an external link, the destination website processes information under its own privacy policy.

### Data Retention and Deletion

Because SnapSaver Camera does not transmit your photos or personal data to the developer's servers, the developer does not retain such data on a server. You can delete photos on your device and remove local app settings by clearing the app's data or uninstalling it.

### Children's Privacy

SnapSaver Camera does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not collect or upload personal data from any user.

### Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. Any update will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
